# How did LVMH became the world's leading luxury group 

## Company Overview

### Executive Summary
LVMH was created in 1987 when Moët Hennessy and Louis Vuitton decided to unify, and Bernaurd Arnault has led the LVMH group since 1989, being the majority shareholder. This unification opened a new pathway for the luxury industry, and currently LVMH is the world leader in luxury having brands in all six luxury sectors. The main mission of LVMH is defined as "the art of crafting dreams", and all products and experiences focus on delivering excellence and meeting the highest ethical, social, and environmental standards. LVMH masterfully is involved in the fevelopment and growth of their maisons, while at the same time ensuring they keep their identity, heritage and unique branding.
